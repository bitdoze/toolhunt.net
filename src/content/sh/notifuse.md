Notifuse gives you full ownership of newsletter infrastructure, from audience management to campaign delivery, without depending on external SaaS lock-in.

## Key Features

- **Campaign Management**: Create and run newsletter sends from your own stack.
- **Subscriber Organization**: Segment audience groups for targeted communication.
- **Automation Workflow Support**: Trigger email flows based on events and rules.
- **Self-Hosted Delivery Control**: Keep message pipeline under your infrastructure policy.
- **Developer-Oriented Setup**: Suitable for teams comfortable with Docker deployment.
- **Data Ownership**: Keep subscriber and campaign data private.

## Why Choose Notifuse?

- You need tighter data governance for email operations.
- You want predictable costs at higher send volume.
- You prefer a customizable platform over fixed SaaS constraints.

## Docker Deployment

Deploy Notifuse with persistent storage and a reliable SMTP/provider setup. Use reverse proxy + TLS for production panel access.

## Getting Started

1. Deploy Notifuse services in Docker.
2. Configure domain and sending infrastructure.
3. Import or create subscriber lists.
4. Launch test campaigns and validate deliverability.
