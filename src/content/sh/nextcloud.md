Nextcloud is a powerful self-hosted solution for file storage and collaboration. It gives you Dropbox-like sync, sharing, calendars, contacts, and office collaboration on infrastructure you control.

## Key Features

- **File sync and sharing**: Desktop and mobile clients with selective sync and public links.
- **Collaboration suite**: Built-in Talk, Calendar, Contacts, and collaborative docs.
- **App ecosystem**: Hundreds of apps for notes, forms, maps, AI, and more.
- **Access control**: Fine-grained sharing permissions, groups, and external storage.
- **Self-hosted privacy**: Keep data on your VPS, NAS, or private cloud.

## Why Choose Nextcloud?

- You want Google Drive / Dropbox features without giving data to a SaaS vendor.
- You need one platform for files, chat, calendar, and collaboration.
- You prefer open-source software with a large community and enterprise options.

## Getting Started

1. Deploy Nextcloud AIO or a Docker Compose stack on your server.
2. Point DNS and enable HTTPS.
3. Create the admin account and configure storage.
4. Install desktop/mobile clients and optional apps.
