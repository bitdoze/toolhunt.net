Voicebox is a local-first AI voice studio that combines ElevenLabs-style synthesis and Wispr Flow-style dictation in one free desktop app. Clone voices, generate speech across multiple engines, dictate into any app, and give MCP-aware agents a voice—all while models and audio stay on your machine.

## Why You’ll Love It
- **Local by default**: Voice data, clones, and captures do not need a cloud subscription.
- **Full voice I/O loop**: Output (TTS/cloning) and input (dictation) live in the same app.
- **Serious engine choice**: Seven TTS backends for quality, speed, multilingual, and expressive speech.
- **Creator-ready tools**: Timeline stories editor, effects pipeline, and multi-voice mixing.
- **Agent friendly**: Talk to MCP-aware agents using voices you own.

## What It Offers
- **Near-perfect cloning**: Zero-shot voice cloning from a few seconds of reference audio.
- **7 TTS engines**: Qwen3-TTS, Qwen CustomVoice, LuxTTS, Chatterbox Multilingual, Chatterbox Turbo, HumeAI TADA, and Kokoro.
- **Dictation into any app**: Global hotkey speech-to-text for system-wide capture.
- **Multilingual synthesis**: Broad language coverage (including 23+ language support depending on engine).
- **Stories editor**: Build multi-voice narratives with timeline tracks and clip mixing.
- **Audio effects**: Pitch shift, reverb, delay, chorus, compression, and filters.
- **Cross-platform desktop**: Native app downloads for macOS, Windows, and Linux.

## Perfect For
- **Creators** producing podcasts, narration, and multi-character stories locally.
- **Privacy-minded users** who want ElevenLabs/Wispr-like power without cloud lock-in.
- **Developers** wiring local voice I/O into agents and automation.
- **Power users** who want one studio for clone, generate, dictate, and refine.

## Get Started
1. **Download** from [voicebox.sh](https://voicebox.sh/) or [GitHub releases](https://github.com/jamiepine/voicebox/releases).
2. **Install** the desktop app for your OS.
3. **Import or clone** a voice from a short sample.
4. **Generate speech** or enable dictation with a global hotkey.
5. **Optional**: Connect MCP agents and use your chosen voice profile.

## System Requirements
- macOS, Windows, or Linux desktop
- Enough local disk/RAM for selected speech models
- Microphone access for dictation/capture workflows
