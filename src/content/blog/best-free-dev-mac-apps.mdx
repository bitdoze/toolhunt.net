## Setting Up Your Development Environment

1. **Choose your core tools**
   - Start with editor + terminal + API client
   - Add database and process tools when your work needs them

2. **Optimize the workflow**
   - Learn keyboard shortcuts early
   - Keep configs consistent across machines
   - Prefer lightweight tools that stay out of the way

3. **Watch performance**
   - Monitor CPU/RAM during heavy local work
   - Update tools regularly
   - Drop utilities that no longer earn their keep

## Conclusion

A free Mac dev environment in 2026 can be fast, modern, and complete. Pick the tools that match your stack, learn them deeply, and expand only when a new job appears in your workflow.
