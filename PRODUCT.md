# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Developers, sysadmins, self-hosters, power users, and tech enthusiasts. They visit ToolHunt to discover curated self-hosted apps, Mac software, online developer tools, and privacy-respecting alternatives, backed by clear deployment guides and direct repository links.

## Product Purpose

ToolHunt is a high-speed static catalog that aggregates self-hosted applications, online tools, Mac utilities, and software alternatives. It cuts through noise, software bloat, and paywalls by providing verified tool cards, instant client-side search, categories, and direct setup guides. Success is measured by fast tool discovery and confident visitor click-throughs to repositories and setup tutorials.

## Positioning

The fastest, cleanest directory for self-hosted apps & modern tools with actionable setup guides.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Click-through to verified external repository or official tool URLs (`checkItUrl`).
- Secondary CTA: Access step-by-step Full Setup Guides on BitDoze and explore curated category alternatives.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "ToolHunt gives me instant access to verified self-hosted tools and apps with direct links and real setup guides, zero fluff."
- Belief ladder:
  1. This directory is fast, developer-first, and curated without spam or bloated ad walls.
  2. The listed tools are active, high-quality, and categorized clearly (Self-Hosted, Mac, Online, Alternatives, AI).
  3. Clicking through leads directly to verified projects or comprehensive setup tutorials.
- Proof on hand: Over 100+ indexed self-hosted and software tool entries, structured schemas, live search, automated CI checks, and BitDoze integration guides.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, efficient, and developer-centric. Voice is authoritative, direct, and concise. Evokes confidence, speed, and clarity.

## Anti-references

Bloated download directories, ad-cluttered spam portals, and generic 2023 AI SaaS templates featuring heavy side-stripe borders, decorative gradient text, oversized pill cards, or fake sketchy SVG illustrations.

## Design Principles

1. **Utility First**: Information density and visual hierarchy must make tool scanning effortless.
2. **Speed & Directness**: Search, filtering, and detail navigation must respond immediately without layout shifts or intrusive overlays.
3. **Verified Quality**: Treat every tool card and screenshot as a proof point—clean typography, clear licensing/pricing tags, and direct links.
4. **Restrained Elegance**: Use subtle borders, purposeful micro-interactions, and refined dark/light contrast instead of loud decorative gimmicks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. High text contrast (≥4.5:1 for body and muted elements), full keyboard navigation for search dialog and filter chips, explicit focus rings (`:focus-visible`), aria labels for filters/modals, and complete `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` motion suppression.
